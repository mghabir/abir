About Me

After my High School, I Joined for BSc Engineering Program at International Islamic University Chittagong, which is an International Standard University maintain the curriculum Standard of top-ranked Engineering University in Bangladesh also with abroad as it has the accreditation of IEB. Currently, I am doing my MSc in Hokkaido University, Japan under Japanese Government (MEXT) Scholarship.

During my Undergraduate starting, I was involved with many International/domestic NGOs for Contributing some good works in my Community. I have begun to realize the value and significance of helping others and contributing to my surrounding Community. In 2017 I was involved with The Wikimedia Education Program as a Volunteer & Worked for ICT enable Education, especially in South Asia. For Project Outreach Program, I visited Srilanka, Thailand, also Japan (ICETC-2018) to promote Open Education on behalf of The Wikimedia Foundation. In 2019 I was selected for Project developer in UNESCO Open Education for a better world. For this project, I was invited to the 9th Pan-Commonwealth Forum in Edinburgh, UK. During these years, I volunteered on many organizations like Creative Commons, Mozilla, Open Knowledge Foundation, TechSoup, Center for Open Sciences. Most of the time, my focus was on establishing Education freer & easily accessible. I tried to increase substantial knowledge of the UN Sustainable Development goals. For my ICT background, it was always helpful to get familiar with new technology & tools, which makes Education easier.
